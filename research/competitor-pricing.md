## Why Did physioshop24.ch and physio-shop.com Leave?

### physioshop24.ch (SVG Medizinsysteme GmbH & Co. KG)

**Who they were:** German company based in M√ºhlacker, Baden-W√ºrttemberg. In business since 1997. Described themselves as "one of the largest online shops for physiotherapy supplies in Switzerland." Had a Swiss PostFinance bank account (IBAN CH15), Swiss domain, Swiss delivery.

**Why they left:** No official statement beyond "Unsere Gesch√§ftst√§tigkeit in der Schweiz wird leider nicht fortgef√ºhrt." (Our business activities in Switzerland will unfortunately not be continued.) Still operating in Germany and Austria.

**Most likely reasons (inferred):**
- Swiss regulatory burden: Foreign companies selling medical devices into Switzerland must comply with MepV (Swissmedic), which as of the 2022/2023 Switzerland-EU divergence requires more than just CE marking. German companies face additional complexity vs. a Swiss-domiciled importer.
- Swiss MWST administration: Separate Swiss VAT registration, reporting, and compliance is operationally costly for a German company where the Swiss market is a small fraction of total revenue.
- Customs overhead: Every shipment from Germany requires a customs declaration. Post-Brexit-equivalent friction for B2B shipments to Switzerland creates cost and delay that erodes margin on low-value consumables.
- Market size vs. overhead: Switzerland = ~9M people. Germany = ~84M. The Swiss operation may simply not have justified the administrative cost.

**The structural insight:** A Swiss-based importer (us) doesn't have any of these problems. We import once from China into Switzerland, comply with Swiss regulations as a Swiss company, charge Swiss MWST natively. This is a structural advantage over German competitors.

Timing (early 2026, same period as physioshop24.ch exit) may suggest broader German-operated CH physio supply businesses are struggling.
