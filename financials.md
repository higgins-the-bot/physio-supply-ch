## 7. The "AcuMax Scenario" — Sensitivity

If AcuMax is already at 50% below our estimates (unlikely but possible):

| | Our price | AcuMax (adjusted) | Our advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tape 5m roll | CHF 5.90 | CHF 4.50 | -CHF 1.40 (we lose) |
| Tape 32m roll | CHF 18.90 | CHF 14 | -CHF 4.90 (we lose) |
| TENS pads | CHF 5.90 | CHF 4.50 | -CHF 1.40 (we lose) |

In this scenario, there's no price-based moat. The business case collapses.

**If AcuMax is at 25% below our estimates (middle ground):**
- We can still price competitively by targeting 15% below AcuMax
- Margin compresses but remains workable (50–60% vs. 74%)

**This is why verifying AcuMax prices is the single most important pre-launch action.**
